                            Even though Overlord is another overpowered main character Isekai, this story distinguishes itself by having exceptional character development.
                            Even though the outcome of every standoff was a foregone conclusion, the detailed feelings and thoughts of the characters make each encounter interesting. As our understanding of each character and the world they've been transported too increases, the depth of the novel and investment in their future grows proportionally.
                            Aside from that, the Overlord Light Novels are very well translated, something which cannot be taken for granted, and reads like the Percy Jackson or Inheritance Series.</p>

                                for the main character as he faces off against death time and time again. For me, the hook
                                and beginning of this book we're the most engaging parts of the manga. As time went on I
                                started to lose interest, and I felt that the deepening of the plot was not enough to revamp
